Income and Employment

Income comes from various sources, including salaries, wages, tips, and commissions, which are earned through active work. The gig economy and freelance work provide flexibility and additional opportunities to earn money but often come without benefits. Passive income, such as rental income, royalties, and dividends, generates money with minimal active effort, making it an important component of long-term financial planning. Understanding your income sources allows you to maximize earnings and create a diversified financial foundation.

Remember This: Your income sources determine your financial stability, so explore ways to diversify them. Passive income can be a powerful tool for building wealth.
